# App Store Privacy Policy

Wyoming Events Calendar uses a single, comprehensive privacy policy that satisfies both **Apple App Store** and **Google Play Store** requirements.

## Privacy Policy URL

**https://casperevents.org/privacy**

Use this URL when submitting the app to:

- **App Store Connect** → App Information → Privacy Policy URL
- **Google Play Console** → Store presence → Store listing → Privacy policy

## In-App Access

The privacy policy is accessible from within the app:

1. Settings tab → Privacy Policy
2. Tap "View Full Privacy Policy (App Store & Play Store)" to open the policy in the browser

## Store-Specific Requirements

### Apple App Store
- Privacy policy URL: https://casperevents.org/privacy
- Complete the **App Privacy** section in App Store Connect (data collection disclosure / nutrition labels)
- Declare: push notification tokens, identifiers, optional account info

### Google Play Store
- Privacy policy URL: https://casperevents.org/privacy
- Complete the **Data safety** form in Play Console
- Declare: device or other IDs, app interactions (push subscriptions)

## Policy Content

The web privacy page at casperevents.org/privacy includes:
- Mobile app-specific section (push tokens, Expo/APNs/FCM, city filter)
- Data we collect and do not collect
- User rights (disable push, unregister device)
- Contact for data deletion requests
